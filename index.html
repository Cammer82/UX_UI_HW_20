FCC Redesign Project

In this project, I redesigned the Homepage and Navigation for the Federal Communications Commission. There were several rounds of testing and iterations to try to narrow down all the government jargon and make the site more usable.

Mindful Me App

My group and I came up with an app that would help busy people, add self-care into their day to day. It was my initial research & interviews that helped us realize which direction we wanted to take with a mindfulness app.

WGEF Redesign

This was a group project where we would redesign the homepage and Navigation, of a Non-Profit of our choice. We chose the Women's Global Empowerment Fund. An organization that helps women and families in Northern Uganda.
