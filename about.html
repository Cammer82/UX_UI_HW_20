Since I was little, I loved to draw and do all the artistic things. I never felt like I belonged in school, except in art class. I LOVED the structure and guidance of learning new ways to create!

After highschool, I stopped creating art for about 20yrs. Then a big life change happened in 2020 (aside from a world pandemic!), that led me back to art. Which allowed my mind to open and heal. From there, I found the courage, to dream of something “more” in life.

And one early morning google search of “graphic design” led me to a UXUI Design Bootcamp. Seven months later, I’m so incredibly excited to learn and grow in this challenging field of UX Design. And hope to continue to learn and grow with every new Design experience!
